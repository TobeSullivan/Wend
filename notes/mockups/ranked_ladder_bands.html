What changed from the last version: nobody's invisible — a Gold player is #34 of 100, not "not on the board." Tiers survive as bands (the durable "I'm Gold" identity + the Platinum milestone to chase), but they no longer gate you off a leaderboard. Band tags use only palette colors — gold/terracotta/sage tints, nothing invented.

For a Silver player it's the identical screen, just centered lower: their green row sits in the Silver band, Bronze shows below, Gold/Plat/Masters above — same "you're #N of 100" legibility.

Knock-on (small): the post-match Ranked panel gains a global-rank line too — "Gold 47→77 · +30 LP · #41 → #34" — so the end screen shows you the places you climbed, not just the LP. Cheap, and it's the satisfying number.
