Wend - Round-end overlay (one system, two payloads)

One overlay, two payloads. It's a transient event over the board - arrives on resolution, holds, then leaves; nothing persistent. Trials pops the gold and score deltas you earned this round. Ranked shows the pairwise lives swing - the net is the hero pop, the per-opponent rows stagger in below (green you took, terracotta you lost). Same arrive / pop / leave vocabulary, same frame - only the contents differ, so it's one thing to build, not two.
