# Parked — additive, not now

Design seeds that are deliberately deferred. Not tasks (those live on the Jira **KAN** board),
not locks (those live in `notes/decisions.md`), not stale. Revisit each only if the trigger
named below actually shows up. Promote to a KAN ticket when a seed graduates to "build it."

---

- **Generic nature-prop obstacle buy.** Fixes the "same few props" repetition on Forest / Beach /
  Summer (the Suburbia pack only covers its own board). Buy deliberately if the alive-levels payoff
  proves out in playtest. Trigger: boards read as visually samey in real play.

- **Individual-while-grouped Trials scoring.** A future option letting grouped players each post to
  the Solo board instead of (or alongside) the team score. Group size is the board for now. Trigger:
  players ask to queue together without sharing a leaderboard line.

- **Ranked ready-check.** Ships OFF (locked in `decisions.md`). Additive only — flip on solely if
  AFK-poisoning shows up in beta. Listed here as the watch item; the lock owns the default.

- **Match reconstruction after coordinator crash.** The match model is re-simmable, but a coordinator
  crash currently voids the match with no LP rather than reconstructing it. Reconstruction is a
  post-launch robustness improvement, not launch-critical (the void-no-LP behavior is locked).
